Ordinary

Word Game

by Thomas Cavalcante

There is a list of 4 letter words sorted in alphabetical order.

Given a random word, guess the next word by thinking alphabetically.

One night with a group of friends, I picked up a dictionary.

Flipped to a random page, chose a random word, and told my friend the random word.

Then I asked him to guess the next word in the dictionary following the random word...

By thinking in alphabetical order.

Try it out ordinarygame.com
